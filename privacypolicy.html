Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 3, 2025

Drive Organizer Pro is a tool that integrates with Google Drive to help users organize their files more efficiently. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data.

What Information We Access

  • We access metadata about your Google Drive files (such as name, type, and folder path) to provide sorting, labeling, and organization features.
  • We do not access the content of your files unless required to rename or move them.

How We Use Your Information

  • All data is processed temporarily within the scope of your session and is not stored, saved, or shared externally.
  • Your information is used only to perform the actions you explicitly trigger (e.g., renaming, moving files).

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

Data Storage

Drive Organizer Pro does not store any user data on our servers or databases. All processing is done within Google Apps Script runtime.

Permissions

Our app requests only the necessary OAuth permissions to perform file operations on your behalf. You may revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, please email us at obliquitymarketing@gmail.com.
