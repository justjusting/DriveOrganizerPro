Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 3, 2025

Drive Organizer Pro is a tool that integrates with Google Drive to help users organize their files more efficiently. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle and protect your data.

What Information We Access

  • We access metadata about your Google Drive files (such as name, type, and folder path) to provide sorting, labeling, and organization features.
  • We do not access the content of your files unless required to rename or move them.

How We Use Your Information

  • All data is processed temporarily within the scope of your session and is not stored, saved, or shared externally.
  • Your information is used only to perform the actions you explicitly trigger (e.g., renaming, moving files).

Data Protection Mechanisms

  • All communications between our app and Google APIs are secured using HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or higher).
  • No sensitive information is ever stored on our servers; all operations occur within Google Apps Scriptâ€™s secure environment.
  • OAuth 2.0 is used for authentication and authorization, ensuring you control access to your account at all times.
  • Access tokens are short-lived and automatically revoked when the session ends.
  • Only the minimum required permissions (OAuth scopes) are requested to perform your requested actions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Data Storage

Drive Organizer Pro does not store any user data on our servers or databases. All processing is done within the secure Google Apps Script runtime.

Permissions

Our app requests only the necessary OAuth permissions to perform file operations on your behalf. You may revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.
