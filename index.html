Drive Organizer Pro

Drive Organizer Pro is a powerful Google Drive Workspace Add-on that helps users manage and organize their Drive files with ease.

Key Features

  • Visual file and folder tree with collapsible folders
  • Drag-and-drop reorganization of files and folders
  • Multi-select actions: Move, Rename, Delete
  • Create new folders instantly
  • Folder picker modal for advanced moves
  • Pro user features managed via Firebase

Why We Request Access

Drive Organizer Pro integrates directly with your Google Drive to perform the actions you initiate. It requests the following permissions:

  • Google Drive Access – To view, move, rename, delete, and create files on your behalf.
  • Email & Profile Info – To identify your Google account and manage your Pro access level.
  • Datastore (Firestore) – To store your app preferences and Pro status securely.

Your data is used solely to perform the tasks you initiate within the app. We do not store or share your files or data with any third parties.
